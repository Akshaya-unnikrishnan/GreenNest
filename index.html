Plant Care Tips

Water Properly

Avoid overwatering and let the soil dry slightly between watering.

Bright Light

Keep your plants in bright indirect sunlight.

Room Temperature

Maintain temperatures between 18°C and 28°C.

Repotting

Repot every 12–18 months to encourage healthy growth.
